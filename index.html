Architecture Portfolio

This portfolio investigates kinetic tension, light, and sustainability through hands-on design. Projects like Dynamic Study: The Rhythm of Basketball translate movement into wireframe structures, while Cave and Timber explores contrasts of mass and void. A sustainability thread reimagines urban waste (paper cups, barbed wire) as architectural elements, bridging artistic vision with engineered precision.

Intuition Literary Magazine

This magazine is my passion project — a fusion of my love for literature, art, and design. Curated and designed entirely for joy, Intuition features works like Jhumpa Lahiri’s meditations on language and Ada Limón’s aching nostalgia, paired with original art and commentary. It frames intuition as both a survival tool and an artistic compass, a testament to trusting our gut.
